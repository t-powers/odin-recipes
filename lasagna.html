The Best Lasagna Recipe - You Must Try It

Prepare

Gather your ingredients and spices.

  1. Lasagna - 1 Box
  2. Large Can Tomato Sauce
  3. Package mozzarella cheese
  4. Spices
    • garlic powder
    • oregano
    • parsley
    • pepper
    • chili pepper

Now, it's time to cook, assemble, and bake your lasagna dish.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. In large pot, fill with water and add salt.
  3. In pan, open tomato sauce and add spices to taste. Bring to simmer.
  4. Get medium oven safe pan and spread sauce on bottom.
  5. When noodles are flexible, not too soft, put single layer on bottom
  6. Spread thin layer of sauce on top. Repeat until noodles and sauce are in dish.
  7. Layer mozzarella cheese on top.
  8. Stick in oven. When edges of cheese turn brown, take out of oven and let it sit for 20 minutes.
  9. Cut into sections and serve. Enjoy!
