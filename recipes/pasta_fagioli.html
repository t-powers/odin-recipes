Pasta Fagioli Soup

Prepare

Gather your ingredients using the list below.

  • Sauce base: Tomato sauce, tomato paste, V8 juice, chicken broth
  • Veggies: celery, diced tomatoes, shredded carrots, crushed garlic
  • Beans: kidney beans, northern beans
  • Pasta: Ditalini
  • Meat: lean ground beef
  • Spices: dried oregano, basil, thyme, marjoram, parsley

Steps

Add a drizzle of olive oil in sauce pan on medium heat, saute the veggies. Next, cook ground beef and drain the fat. Add spices to ground beef or veggies while cooking. Cook pasta until al dante.

In large pot, add beans, sauce base, veggies, and meat. Bring to boil mild boil and let simmer for 30 minutes. Add pasta to last 10 minutes of simmering.

Serve in a bowl with soft warm bread.
